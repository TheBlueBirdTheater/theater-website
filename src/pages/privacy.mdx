## Overview

{OPTP_NAME}, the 501(c)(3) nonprofit that operates the {THEATRE_NAME}, respects your privacy. This
policy describes what information we collect through this website and how we use it. We do not sell
or rent your personal information to third parties.

## Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name and email address when you
sign up for our newsletter, or contact information submitted through our contact form. When you
purchase tickets, that transaction is handled by our third-party ticketing provider under its own
privacy policy — we do not collect or store your payment information.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to send newsletters and show announcements (only if you've signed
up), respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form, and understand how visitors use this
site so we can improve it.

## Third-Party Services

This site links out to third-party services, including our ticketing provider and our email
newsletter provider. Once you leave this site to use one of those services, your information is
subject to that provider's own privacy policy, not this one.

## Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent to <a href={`mailto:${contact.email}`}>{contact.email}</a>, or see
our [Contact](/contact) page for other ways to reach us.
